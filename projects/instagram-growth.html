Instagram Growth Campaign

Project Overview

Launched an organic growth strategy to increase brand visibility and community engagement on Instagram using reels, stories, and interactive posts.

Tactics Used:

  • Created short-form Reels and carousel posts
  • Used trending hashtags and audio
  • Hosted weekly story polls and Q&As
  • Optimized profile bio, highlights, and CTA links

Results:

  • +60% increase in follower count in 60 days
  • Engagement rate rose from 3.2% to 6.1%
  • Boosted website traffic from Instagram by 40%
